About

Jonathan Dumas is a PhD candidate in weather based forecasting and decision making for microgrids at the University of Liège (Belgium). Previously, he held positions in several compagnies of the energy field, see more at Linkedin

His research is at the intersection of machine learning and power systems. TODO His present research interests include:

  • probabilistic forecasting applied to power systems: PV, wind power, consumption, electricity prices ...
  • stochastic and robust optimization
  • deep generative models applied to power systems: VAE, GAN, NF ...
  • local energy communities
  • energy management systems
